ABOUT THE GAME

A game where there's more to the world than just what you can see.

Light Between is currently in development, but here are three things to know...

RESTORE THE LIGHT OF THE LAND

Puzzle Platformer

Explore a mystical world guided by light and shadows through platforming, puzzles, and other obstacles. The light of the land has been lost, and it's up to you to find it.

COLLECT ABILITIES

More Than Running & Jumping

Different areas of the land can only be reached with new abilities. Sparks of light hold new powers that will assist your journey through the lands.

UNIQUE MECHANICS

Using Your Shadow

No light is without a shadow, and in The Light Between, your shadow becomes your most important tool on your adventure. Use it to complete puzzles, reach new areas, and explore.

ABOUT THE DEVELOPER

A little bit about Madi Bee

What started as a school project...

The Light Between has become my personal passion project after creating its protoype as my final for my Intro to Unreal course at Louisiana State University. The game is a nod to my admiration for games with unique styles and mechanics such as Ori and the Will of the Wisps, Little Nightmares, and even Super Mario Bros. Not only that, but I've also had such a fasination with lighting and how it impacts a game, so I thought "why not make a game that actually uses that light for its mechanics?". I've been using this game as a way for me to learn and experiment with all that goes into game creation (coding and blueprinting, concept design, animation, modeling, level design, and so much more).
